About Raftaar

Raftaar is a modern intercity bus company focused on safe, comfortable and on‑time journeys across Pakistan. Our fleet combines premium coaches, experienced drivers and smart technology to give you a seamless ride.

Started in 2015 with just two luxury buses on the Islamabad–Lahore route, Raftaar has grown into a network connecting major cities such as Karachi, Peshawar and Multan, carrying thousands of passengers every month.

Every bus offers reclining seats, powerful air‑conditioning, charge ports and professional staff, so families, students and business travellers can trust Raftaar for their next journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive at the terminal?

▶

Passengers are advised to arrive at least 30 minutes before departure to complete check‑in and luggage tagging comfortably.

Why can’t I proceed to payment?

▶

Ensure you’ve selected at least one seat and chosen a gender for each selected seat. The system blocks payment if any selected seat is missing a gender.

Which payment methods are accepted?

▶

We currently support Card payments. Loyalty tokens can be redeemed to reduce your fare during checkout.

How do refunds work?

▶

Request a refund from your bookings list. Once approved, your seat is released back to availability and the refund is recorded with your provided account details.

What are loyalty tokens and how do I redeem them?

▶

You earn 1 token for every 5 trips. Each token gives you a Rs. 50 discount at checkout. Check your tokens in the Loyalty section and apply them during payment.

Can I change my username, email or password?

▶

Yes. Go to your Profile to update credentials or password. Changes overwrite the old values and take effect immediately for login.

Can I choose seat gender later?

▶

Gender must be chosen before payment. You can select seats first and assign gender via the selector beside each seat number.
